The Autobiographical Game

A delve into the mind of a below average human

Assignment#7 - The final project for Critical Computation is an autobiographical game divided into three stages with different gameplay mechanics reflecting the various aspects of my personality. The levels include a space battle acting as a metaphor for writer's block, a fighting game parody about my mental health struggles, and a platformer themed around my social life. The entire game is scored with 8-bit renditions of songs I have written, the lyrics of which flash perodically across the screen.
*HIGHLY recommended to play with a windows laptop or desktop viewport
