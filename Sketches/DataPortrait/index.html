Data Portrait

Assignment#6 A virtual iPod that documents my Spotify listening history over a two-day period. It has complete clickwheel scrolling functionality, and all the buttons work as they do on the original device. The sketch plays a selection of tracks from the top artists I listened to during that two day period, and utilizes p5's FFT algorithms to generate a responsive waveform of the audio being played.
