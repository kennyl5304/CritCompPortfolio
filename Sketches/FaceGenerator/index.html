Face Generator

Assignment#2 - Inspired by cooperative climbing video game Peak, I created a face standing in front of a rocky mountain range that changes size based on the position of the user's mouse. With every click of the mouse, the size of the eye pupils, thickness of the eyebrows, and colour of the face gets randomized. Background soundtrack composed in Chrome Music Lab.
