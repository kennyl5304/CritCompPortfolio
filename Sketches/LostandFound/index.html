Lost and Found

Assignment#1 - The Lost and Found assignment involved sketching a scene based on a prompt given. I received the description from my partner of "A log cabin in a snowy pine forest" and immediately conjured up the image in my head of the cabin not as a centerpiece, but rather a part of a much larger scene. The sketch above depicts the scene of a snowy path leading to a log cabin situated alongside multiple pine trees scattered throughout the backdrop of a jagged snowy mountain range, additionally decorated by animated snowfall.
