Exquisite Corpse

Assignment#4 - The exquisite corpse assignment was a collaborative task between me, Anna Zimmer, and Zainab Vajihi where the three of us exchanged drawings of figures and created a creature mixing parts from all three. The final product for me combined the head from mine, which was inspired by the cover art of Harlan Ellison's I Have No Mouth and I Must Scream, as well as King Crimson's In the Court of the Crimson King, Anna's torso, and Zainab's legs. The sketch above depicts the final creature as a bat-harpy hybrid with its lower half merged with electronic components linked to a cassette and a chilli pepper. The cassette on it's leg is also playable.
